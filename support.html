Vitta — Support

Diet, workout and lifestyle tracking.

Need help? Email rodrigobastosv@gmail.com and you'll get a reply within a few days. Including your device model and iOS version makes it much faster to sort out.

Common questions

Do I need an account?

No. Vitta works from the moment you open it, with everything saved to your device's own anonymous session. Creating an account is only needed to subscribe, or to keep your data if you change phones.

What does Premium include?

The two AI photo-scanning features: photographing a meal to log every item at once, and scanning a nutrition label to fill in the macros. Everything else — food search, the catalog, recipes, water, sleep, body weight, workouts, reminders and every history chart — is free and stays free.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by us: open the Settings app, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Cancelling stops the renewal and you keep access until the end of the period you have already paid for.

I subscribed but Premium isn't unlocked

Open the Premium screen and tap Restore purchases. If it still doesn't unlock, make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you subscribed with, and into the same Vitta account. Email us if it persists.

Why does the meal scan sometimes get it wrong?

Portion sizes are estimated from a photo, so they're a starting point rather than a measurement. Every detected item is editable before it's logged — adjust the grams, or untick anything that isn't yours.

Sleep isn't importing from Apple Health

Vitta needs read permission for sleep data. Check Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Vitta and make sure sleep is enabled. Nothing is ever written back to Health.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app: Profile → Delete account. It's immediate and permanent, and removes everything you've logged. See the Privacy Policy for the detail of what's kept and why.

Report a bug or request a feature
